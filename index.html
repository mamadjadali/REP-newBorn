Frequently Asked Questions

What is the goal of network security in a company or organization?

Network security should include components such as protecting against unauthorized access, ensuring data integrity, and safeguarding against network threats.

What's the Definition of vulnerability, threat, and risk in the context of network security?

Vulnerability refers to flaws or violations in the system or hardware, while threat is the method by which an incident is caused, and risk is the increase in the likelihood of a threat resulting from a secure but not sufficiently protected system.

What are the possible consequences of a computer network attack?

Consequences may include data breaches, financial losses, damage to reputation, and legal implications.

What are the best ways to protect yourself from a brute force login attack?

Implementing strong password policies, using multi-factor authentication, and employing Network Intrusion Detection Systems (NIDS) are effective measures.
